Setup

Volume check

Click play to hear the background sound used in this study. Adjust your device volume until the chatter is clearly audible but comfortable — like background noise in a café where you can hear people talking but can't make out words.

Not playing
Once set, do not adjust your volume for the rest of the test. The sounds will vary in character between conditions — that is intentional.
Practice — Part 1 of 2
Stroop practice

A color word will appear on screen. Click the button matching the ink color — not what the word says.

Example:

BLUE

Correct answer: Red — because the ink is red, not the word.

Practice results are not recorded. Go as fast and accurately as you can.
Practice — Part 2 of 2
2-back practice

Letters will appear one at a time. Each time a letter appears, decide: does it match the letter from 2 steps ago?

Example sequence:

B K B T K

The highlighted letters match what appeared 2 steps earlier. Click Match when it matches, No match when it doesn't.

You only start responding from the 3rd letter onward. The first 2 are shown automatically.
